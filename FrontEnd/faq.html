Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can donate?
A: Anyone with surplus food — restaurants, caterers, households, etc.

Q: Is there a minimum quantity to donate?
A: No, every contribution counts. Even a few meals can help someone in need.

Q: How is food collected?
A: Once you submit a donation, our team or partner NGOs will coordinate pickup based on your location.

Q: Is there any cost involved?
A: No, using the platform and contributing food is completely free.

Q: What type of food can I donate?
A: Freshly cooked meals, packaged items, and non-perishables that are safe to eat. Avoid expired or spoiled items.

Q: How can someone request food?
A: Click on “Request Food” on the homepage and fill in your details. We’ll get in touch soon!

Q: How do you ensure food safety?
A: We request donors to follow hygiene practices. Our volunteers inspect food before distributing.

Q: Can I volunteer for this cause?
A: Absolutely! Reach out through our Contact section and we’ll connect with you.
